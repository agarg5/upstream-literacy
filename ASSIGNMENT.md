## Two Project Options

### Option 1: Community for Literacy Leaders With Search and Matching Tool (AI-Solution)

**What it is:** A multi-level community site that matches literacy leaders based on defined criteria, helping school district administrators find peers facing similar challenges.

**Problem:** School district leaders working on literacy need to connect with others in similar situations, but there's no tool that combines demographic data with the specific challenges they face.

**Functional Requirements:**
- Users enter the community and sort/filter who they engage with based on criteria specific to their situation
- **Demographic matching** (auto-ingested from public sources):
  - District type (urban, suburban, rural)
  - District size
  - Free and reduced lunch rates
  - English as a second language population
- **Problem statement matching** (self-selected from pre-set list):
  - Developer creates a curated list of key non-demographic drivers of literacy success
  - Users select which problems they face (e.g., "implementation of a new curriculum tool")
  - System matches users with others who selected the same problems
- Users can identify and message other members based on matching criteria
- Company-side moderation functionality to enter into conversations

**Required Languages:** TBD by developer

### Option 2: E-commerce Shopping Cart & Checkout System (AI-Accelerated)

**What it is:** A modern e-commerce platform to replace phone/email ordering.

**Problem:** Company lacks modern e-commerce presence. Manual order processing requires 2-3 hours daily. Results in abandoned sales, delayed fulfillment, inventory discrepancies.

**Functional Requirements:**
- Product catalog with categories, images, detailed descriptions
- Shopping cart with add/remove/quantity updates and persistent sessions
- Secure checkout with guest and registered user options
- Payment processing (credit card and PayPal)
- Order management with status tracking and email confirmations
- Inventory management with stock level tracking and out-of-stock handling
- Customer account creation with order history and profile management

**Required Languages:** Python (Django/Flask), JavaScript (React/Vue.js), HTML/CSS
